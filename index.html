Who we are

We're a competitive robotics team run by high school students based in Piedmont, California. Together, we focus on working as a team to build a robot and having a great high school experience. We were founded in 2009, making us the oldest team in Piedmont and 5th oldest in Northern California as of the 2021-2022 season.

Want to join?

The traditional recruiting season is at the beginning of the school year. However, we're happy to accept new members at any time. If you're interested in having a great time building robots (as well as a good resume for college), feel free to apply.

Contact Us!

If you have any questions about joining or anything else related to the team, feel free to get in touch.
