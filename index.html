Who we are

We are a competitive robotics team run by high school students based in Piedmont, California. Together, we focus on working as a team to build a robot in addition to having a good high school experience. We were founded in 2009, making us the oldest team in Piedmont, and in the 2021-2022 season we were the 5th oldest team in Northern California.

Interested in joining?

The tradidtional recruiting season is at the beginning of the school year, but we are happy to accept new members any time of the year. If you're interested in having a great time building robots (as well as looking good for colleges), feel free to apply
